Chhattisgarh is one of the ancient states of India. Chhattisgarh borders associated with the states of Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, and Telangana. The Chhattisgarhi culture is immersed in varieties of dance forms, mainly due to the number of tribes residing in the vast expanse of the state.
